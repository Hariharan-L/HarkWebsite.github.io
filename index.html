PC-Gaming

A stronghold of reliability and the most comfortable secluded area where you can play the latest games (if the hardware allows).Improved graphics every year. It shows wonders of realism – lighting and weather effects, particle effects, ray tracing, reflections, and so on. Revenue from the global PC games market was estimated at nearly $40 billion, while the mobile games market generated a revenue of over $92 billion. If we consider the full picture of mobile vs PC vs console statistics, then we must also mention that the consoles segment came in second with $51 billion.

The presence of multiplayer, which only warmed the general fan more – the ability to play with real people, communicate, and work in teams immerses people in the game world with their heads.Huge monitors, surround sound, and convenient controls remove all barriers between the virtual and real worlds.

Mobile-Gaming

Your smartphone is always with you – you can play in transport, during a lunch break, or while waiting for an order in a cafe.Modern games are perfectly optimized and adapt to any device, so playing them is a pleasure.Mobile devices and their RAM have become much more powerful – gaming smartphones are commonplace.

Famous games like Call of Duty recognized the incredible power of the mobile market long ago and brought their hit to mobile devices.You will find your favorite games, multiplayer, and everything your heart desires on your smartphone. The controls, of course, are significantly inferior to the PC, but you have to pay for portability.

2D games, as their name suggests, are titles with only two axes of motion.2D games are flat, sprite-based experiences that only allow you to move up, down, left, and/or right across the screen. 2D games don’t have to be complicated.
3D games, by contrast, include full movement through three-dimensional planes. This means that the player is able to move around in a "real world" setting where they can turn 360 degrees, and in which objects have length, height, and depth. An example is Super Mario Odyssey
Interactive storytelling has been a popular choice in gaming for forever. And we’ve seen the gaming industry do anything and everything to draw players into the games and worlds they create. VR (virtual reality) is the next level of immersion in video games.
